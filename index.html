RESIDENCE FEATURES

This one-bedroom residence includes an Entrance hall/cloakroom, utility cupboard, coffee/champagne bar, reception room, dining area, kitchen, master bedroom with en-suite bathroom, powder room, state of the art security system, a large storeroom, wine cellar, underground parking space at basement level, and terrace access to One Hyde Park’s extensive facilities and amenities are available exclusively to residents. RESIDENCE SUMMARY

Interior designed by Candy & Candy, with Italian marble bathroom and extensive wardrobe space, this one-bedroom residence is one of the finest of its kind in the building, overlooking the trees of the planted courtyard below and on to a city view of Knightsbridge beyond.
